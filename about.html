Why "66" and why "Project"?

Focus on habits.

Our habits define who we are today and who we will become. We have all heard that it takes 21 days to establish a habit. But how long does it take to sustain a habit? You guessed it, it takes 66 days for a habit to become sustainable and cause lifelong changes.

Our mission is to not only help everyone establish healthy habits but to create a system where these habits are sustainable to help us become the best version of ourselves.

A lifelong project.

If you look up "project" on Google, it is defined as "an individual or collaborative enterprise that is carefully planned and designed to achieve a particular aim." To help everyone establish healhty habits, our team is carefully planning and designing the framework to make that possible. We want the framework to be both efficient and successful on a consistent basis.

This project goes beyond our misison. Our lives should be seen as a lifelong project. We must carefully design our lives to reach the all the goals we set. With a strong foundation in healthy habits, we are able to bridge the gap between our mental and physical health. By taking care of our minds and body, we are the best versions of ourselves causing us to become capable of acheiving anything we set our hearts and minds too.

Why is there so much purple?

It's royal purple to be exact.

The color purple is associated with luxury, royalty, power, ambition, and nobility. It is also associated with spirituality, higher self, fulfillment, and passion. Purple fuses the calm stability of blue with the fiece power of red. Purple represents meanings of creativity, dignity, peace, wisdom, wealth, and devotion.

Purple aligns directly with what The 66 Project stands for. To become the best version of yourself you must be willing to commit your time and energy, the two greatest luxuries that you own. You must devote those luxuries to yourself by taking care of your physical and mental wellbeing. Your habits demonstrate qualities of calmness through mediation and fierceness through exercise. A strong foundation in these habits will spark new forms of creativity while bringing peace, fulfillment, and passion to your life.
