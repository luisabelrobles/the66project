Exercise

Daily habit:

Elevating your heart rate.

Get your daily exercise in, minimum 45 minutes to get the benefits. Whether you are following our program or going for a run, just do it. Do not give yourself a reason not to do it.

Benefits

Physical

Weight loss.

When you exercise, you burn calories. The more you burn, the more you lose (nutrition is key).

Daily execise increases your metabolism, which helps sustain healthy weight.

Builds/maintains muscles and bones.

Exercising releases hormones that promote amino acid absorption, which helps them rebuild/grow (yay).

As you age, your bones deteriorate. Daily exercise slows down this process by reducing muscle loss.

Increased energy levels.

Studies show that regular exercise increases energy levels in those who are suffering from an illness and healthy.

Think about all the things you can do in a day if you just exercise!

Reduces risk of chronic disease.

Exercise lowers blood pressure and blood fat levels.

It reduces belly fat! Gut health is just as important as brain health.

Better sex life.

Exercise strengthens the heart, improves blood circulation, encahnces flexibilty, and tones muscles which imporves your sex life! Wow!

Improves sexual performance and plesaure. Studies show women orgasm more frequently and men perfrom better. A win/win!

(We do not promote sexual activity, we are just informing you. If you are sexaully active please check yourself frequently).

Physiological (in other words your brain)

Combats depression and anxiety.

Exercise sparks changes in the brain, reduces inflammation, grows new neurons, and it releases endorphins (the happy hormone).

It alters brain actvity patterns that help you feel calm and accomplished while serving as a distraction from negative emotions.

Relieves stress.

Think about how you feel when you are stressed. Your body is tense leaving you with muscle pain. Thinking about stress makes you more stressed.

Exercise releases all the physcial tension in your body. By releasing physucal tension, your mind will feel beter.

Sharper memory/thinking.

The endorphins that make you feel happier also helps you concentrate and feel mentally sharp.

Exercise stimulates the growth of new brain cells, which prevents age-related decline.

Higher self-esteem.

Exercise helps you feel strong and powerful. This translates over to your other everyday tasks.

Look good, feel good. You will feel better about how you look, become more confident, and unstoppable.

Better sleep.

Exercise helps you regulate your sleep patterns. It improves your sleep quality and increases sleep duration.

If you love sleep, why wouldn't you want to make it better by simply exercising?
